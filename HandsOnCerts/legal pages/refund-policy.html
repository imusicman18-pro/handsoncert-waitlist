Our Policy

HandsOnCert LLC offers a 7-day refund window from the date of purchase on all paid plans — Single Cert ($49) and All-Access Pass ($199).

If you're not satisfied with your purchase for any reason, email us within 7 days of your purchase date and we will issue a full refund — no questions asked.

After the 7-day window has passed, purchases are non-refundable. This applies regardless of how much of the course content you have completed.

Why 7 Days

Seven days from purchase gives you enough time to explore the platform, start Project 1, and decide whether it's right for you — without opening an indefinite refund window that doesn't reflect the nature of digital course access.

Once you've had a week with the product, you have enough information to make that call. We think that's fair.

How to Request a Refund

Email support@handsoncert.com with:

  • The email address associated with your account
  • Your purchase date
  • A brief note that you'd like a refund

We will confirm your refund request and process it within 1 business day. The refund will appear on your original payment method within 5–10 business days depending on your bank or card issuer.

Free tier: The Free plan requires no payment and therefore has no refund policy. You can stop using the free tier at any time with no action required.

Chargebacks

If you believe you have been charged in error, please contact us at support@handsoncert.com before initiating a chargeback with your bank. We respond quickly and almost all billing issues can be resolved directly without involving your card issuer.

Initiating a chargeback without contacting us first may result in account suspension while the dispute is in process.
